How It
Works

01

Enquire & Book

Contact us by phone or email to check availability and confirm your dates. We'll set up your rental agreement.

02

Inspect & Sign

Pick up the truck, complete a vehicle condition report, and sign the rental agreement before departing.

03

Drive & Deliver

Use the truck for your job. Comply with NSW road laws. All tolls and fines during hire are the renter's responsibility.

04

Return & Bond Back

Return the truck clean, undamaged, and with a full tank. Bond refunded after inspection — usually same day.
