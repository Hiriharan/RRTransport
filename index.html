Process

How It
Works

01

Enquire & Book

Contact us by phone or email to check availability and confirm your dates. We'll set up your rental agreement.

02

Inspect & Sign

Pick up the truck, complete a vehicle condition report, and sign the rental agreement before departing.

03

Drive & Deliver

Use the truck for your job. Comply with NSW road laws. All tolls and fines during hire are the renter's responsibility.

04

Return & Bond Back

Return the truck clean, undamaged, and with a full tank. Bond refunded after inspection — usually same day.

Rental Terms

Terms &
Conditions

Carefully read the vehicle description before hire and inspect the truck thoroughly before departure.

Any damage caused during the hire period will result in a damage fee of $5,000.

Late returns must be communicated at least 2 hours prior to the agreed return time. Additional charges apply.

Vehicle must be returned clean, undamaged, and with a full fuel tank.

The hirer is responsible for all tolls, fines, and damage during the rental period.

Bond will be refunded after inspection and clearance of any outstanding charges.

The truck must be returned to the same location it was picked up from.

The driver must present a valid physical driver's licence at the time of truck pickup.

Failure to comply with the above terms and conditions may result in legal action.

The renter must comply with all NSW road laws and company safety policies at all times.
